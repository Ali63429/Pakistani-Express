History
A Rich History

Constructed as a tomb around 2580–2560 BC for the Egyptian Pharaoh Khufu, the Great Pyramid is a testament to ancient Egypt's architectural genius and ingenuity.

Architecture
Architectural Mastery

The pyramid was originally built at 146.6 meters tall, with an original casing of highly polished white limestone meant to reflect the sun’s rays and make the pyramid shine like a jewel.

Exploration
Explore Inside

The interior chambers of the pyramid are open to visitors, including the Queen’s Chamber, the Grand Gallery, and the awe-inspiring King’s Chamber with its granite sarcophagus.
